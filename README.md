# my-vocab-register
📖 Vocab Register — Your Personal Word Collection

> *"I came, I read, I registered."* 📚✨

---

## 🤔 What Is This?

You know that feeling when you're reading a book and stumble across a word that makes you go *"wait... what does THAT mean?"* — and then 5 minutes later you've already forgotten it?

**Not anymore.**

Vocab Register is your personal dictionary companion that:
- 🔍 Looks up ANY word (from "hello" to "solipsistic")
- 🗣️ Handles slang too (yes, even "yeet")
- 💾 Saves it to YOUR personal collection
- 🔤 Keeps everything organized A → Z
- 📊 Tracks your progress like a word-collecting game

---

## 🎮 How It Works (It's Stupid Simple)

```
Step 1: You read a book 📖
Step 2: You find a weird word 🧐
Step 3: You type it in 💬
Step 4: You get the meaning + examples ✅
Step 5: You save it 💾
Step 6: You're now smarter 🧠
```

That's it. That's the app.

## 🔍 Two Modes

| Button | What It Does | Best For |
|--------|-------------|----------|
| 📖 **Define** | Gives you proper dictionary definitions with examples | Books, essays, articles |
| 🗣️ **Slang** | Looks up informal/internet language from Urban Dictionary | Memes, texting, Gen Z speak |

---

## 🧠 Where Does It Get Definitions?

Three sources, in order:

1. **Free Dictionary API** — your standard Oxford-style definitions
2. **Wiktionary** — the backup brain (catches advanced words that #1 misses)
3. **Urban Dictionary** — for when you need to know what "no cap" means

---

## 🔒 Privacy

- Your words are stored in YOUR browser only
- Nobody else can see them
- Sharing the link? They get a blank app — your data stays yours
- No accounts, no logins, no tracking

---

## 📱 Setup (iPhone)

1. Host the `index.html` file on GitHub Pages
2. Open the URL in Safari
3. Tap Share → Add to Home Screen
4. Done! It works like an app

---

## 📱 Pro Tips

- **Add to Home Screen** — feels like a real app
- **Use it while reading** — look up words in real time
- **Don't clear Safari data** — that's where your words live
- **Drag the ❓ button** — put it wherever you want on screen
- **Share freely** — everyone gets their own blank register
