📖 How To Use

"I came, I read, I registered." ✨

🔍 Step 1 — Find a Word

Type any word you've stumbled across while reading.

Two buttons, two vibes:

  • 📖 Define — proper dictionary definitions + real usage examples
  • 🗣️ Slang — informal, internet-speak, Gen Z energy

💡 Can't find a word? The app tries multiple dictionaries automatically!

💾 Step 2 — Save It

Found a definition you like? Tap Save to Register and it's yours forever.

  • ✅ Stored alphabetically, A → Z
  • 🔒 100% private — lives only on YOUR device
  • 🚫 No accounts, no logins, no tracking

📚 Step 3 — Browse Your Collection

Tap My Words to admire your growing vocabulary:

  • 🔤 Filter by letter — highlighted = words saved
  • 🗑️ Remove words you no longer need
  • 📅 See when you added each word

🏆 Step 4 — Level Up

Tap Progress for the fun stuff:

  • 🔥 Day Streaks — add a word daily, watch the fire grow
  • 🌱→🧠 Milestones — unlock badges as you grow
  • 📈 Monthly Chart — see your learning patterns

✨ Pro Tips

📱 Add to Home Screen — opens full-screen like a real app

🖐️ Drag the ❓ button — put it wherever feels right

🔄 Share the link — friends get their own blank register

⚠️ Don't clear Safari data — that's where your words live!

📶 Works offline — browse saved words without internet
