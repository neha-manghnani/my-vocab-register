📖 How to Use

🔍 Looking Up Words

1

Type a word

Enter any word or phrase in the search box on the Look Up tab.

2

Choose your source

📖 Define — for standard dictionary definitions (basic to advanced vocabulary).
🗣️ Slang — for informal terms, internet slang, and colloquialisms.

3

Save it

Tap 💾 Save to Register to add the word to your personal collection.

📚 Browsing Your Words

1

Open My Words tab

Tap the 📚 My Words tab to see your full collection.

2

Filter by letter

Tap any highlighted letter to show only words starting with that letter. Tap All to see everything.

3

Remove words

Tap the 🗑️ icon next to any word to delete it from your register.

💡 Tips

📱 Add to Home Screen — In Safari, tap Share → "Add to Home Screen" for app-like access.

🔒 Your words are private — Saved only on this device. No one else can see them.

⚠️ Don't clear Safari data — This will erase your saved vocabulary.

📶 Works offline — Browsing saved words works without internet. Only new lookups need a connection.

🔗 Safe to share — If you share the link, others get their own empty register. Your words stay yours.
